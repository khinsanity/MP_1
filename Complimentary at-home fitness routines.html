Improved Cardiovascular Health:
The cardio exercises like jumping jacks, high knees, and mountain climbers can help increase your cardiovascular endurance, leading to a healthier heart and improved lung function.
Strength and Muscle Tone:
Regularly performing bodyweight squats, push-ups, tricep dips, and other strength exercises will help build and tone your muscles, giving you a more sculpted appearance.
Core Strength:
The plank and bicycle crunches target your core muscles, which can result in better posture, reduced lower back pain, and enhanced stability.
